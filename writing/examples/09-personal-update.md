# Worked Example: Personal Update

> Format reference: `references/storytelling.md` § 9.
> Medium: LinkedIn announcement post (~150 words).
> Beats hit: Hook → Context → The Update → Rationale.

---

I need to tell you something: I'm shutting down the agency.

For the past 4 years I've been running a 6-person creative shop. As of next month, I'm winding it down by July.

It wasn't easy. The agency was paying my mortgage, and 4 of those 6 people are people I personally hired and care about. We're placing them well — that's the only reason I can do this.

The reason is simple: I started consulting solo six months ago for a quarter of the headcount and double the take-home, and I realized I'd built myself a job, not a business.

At the end of the day, my Saturdays matter more to me than the agency multiple ever will.

If you're hiring a senior strategist or producer in NYC, DM me — I have four people I'd vouch for personally.

---

**Why this works:** behind-the-curtain candor, real tension acknowledged ("4 of those 6 people are people I personally hired"), and a single clean rationale instead of a list of justifications. The DM-me ending turns the announcement into useful action for the reader.
