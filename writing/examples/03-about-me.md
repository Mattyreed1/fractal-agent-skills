# Worked Example: About Me (Origin Story)

> Format reference: `references/storytelling.md` § 3.
> Medium: LinkedIn About post / pinned post (~170 words).
> Beats hit: Intro → Conflict → Epiphany → Change → Purpose.

---

Hi I'm Mara, and three years ago I was a normal corporate lawyer billing 70-hour weeks at a firm in Chicago.

Living a normal life. Nice apartment, dinners I never made it home for, a partner track that always seemed one year out.

Then one Tuesday I got the email — partner offer denied for the second year running. No clear reason. Just "next cycle."

That moment made me understand the firm wasn't going to value the work I cared about, no matter how many hours I billed.

So I quit on a Friday with $11K saved and one client who said yes.

Fast forward to today — I run a boutique practice helping Black women founders structure their first equity raise without paying $20K to a Big Law firm to do it.

Now everything I do is about helping founders who've been ignored by the legacy firms get clean cap tables on their first round.

---

**Why this works:** low-status normalcy → specific trigger event → epiphany in their own words → bold action → mission. Builds personal trust before any expertise claim, which is what the source flags as About Me's purpose.
