# Worked Example: Goal/Dream Journey

> Format reference: `references/storytelling.md` § 5.
> Medium: LinkedIn build-in-public post (~170 words).
> Beats hit: Introduce Dream → Pursuit Of Dream → Progress Update → CTA.

---

Ever since I was 9 watching my dad miss my piano recital because of a layover in Atlanta, I've dreamed of buying him a private jet hour for his 70th.

He turns 70 in 14 months.

A month ago I finally stopped talking about it and quoted my first three retainer clients. I won't lie, I was terrified — I'd never charged that much for my own work and I almost backed down on the call.

Here's where I'm at:

– Consulting practice launched
– $8K MRR (need $15K to fund the goal at this pace)
– $11K of the $30K target banked
– Two more retainers signing in May

Next: I need to land one $5K/mo client by July or this whole thing slips a year.

I'm going to document the entire thing — wins, losses, the calls I bomb. Follow along and hold me accountable. If I don't pull this off, you'll know.

---

**Why this works:** the dream is specific and emotionally legible (one anchor scene from age 9), there is a real numerical target with a deadline that could fail, and the post invites the reader to be a witness. The vulnerability ("if I don't pull this off, you'll know") is the secret sauce the source flags.
