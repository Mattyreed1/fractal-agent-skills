# Worked Example: Win (Victory Announcement)

> Format reference: `references/storytelling.md` § 7.
> Medium: LinkedIn launch-day post (~110 words).
> Beats hit: Hook → Proof → Emotional Beat → Acknowledgment → CTA.

---

It finally happened. We hit $100K MRR.

[Stripe screenshot]

I almost shut this down in February when we churned three anchor accounts in one week. This is why I didn't.

18 months ago we were at $9K MRR and I was paying salaries out of savings. None of this happens without my co-founder Dani showing up every single day.

Next goal: $250K by year-end. Follow to watch.

---

**Why this works:** spontaneous-feeling, undeniable visual proof (a Stripe screenshot, not a vague chart), short emotional acknowledgment without becoming a memoir, and the next-goal CTA channels the energy forward instead of resting on the win.
