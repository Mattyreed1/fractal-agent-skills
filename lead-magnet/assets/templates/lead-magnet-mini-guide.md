# Template: Mini-Guide Lead Magnet

## What This Is
A 3-5 page framework document that teaches ONE thing completely. The reader should be able to implement the framework independently after reading.

## When to Use
The conversation contains a deep framework, system, or methodology with enough substance for extended treatment. Look for:
- Multi-step frameworks with nuance at each step
- War stories with extractable lessons
- Before/after transformations with enough detail to replicate
- Technical setups that need walkthrough-level explanation

## Structure

```markdown
# {Title}
{Subtitle — the specific outcome}

By the user · fractalai.tech

---

## The Problem
{Why this matters. What most people get wrong. What it costs them.}
{2-3 paragraphs. Specific examples. Real stakes.}

## The Framework
### Step 1: {Name}
{What to do, why it matters, specific example}

### Step 2: {Name}
{What to do, why it matters, specific example}

### Step 3: {Name}
{What to do, why it matters, specific example}

{3-5 steps total. Each step must be actionable and include an example.}

## In Practice
{War story or case study showing the framework in action.}
{Specific numbers: timeline, before/after metrics, tools used.}
{What went wrong and how it was fixed.}

## Next Steps
{What the reader should do first.}
{Where to go for more depth — newsletter, service, community.}

---
Built by the user · fractalai.tech
```

## Title Formula
`How to {Outcome} with {Method/Tool} — A {Audience} Guide`

Or: `The {Name} Framework: {Outcome} in {Timeframe}`

Examples:
- "How to Build Your First AI Agent Workflow — A Solo Founder's Guide"
- "The Control Loop Framework: Reliable Agent Operations in 5 Steps"
- "From Manual to Automated: Replacing 10 Hours/Week of Ops Work with n8n"

## Quality Checks

| Check | Pass | Fail |
|-------|------|------|
| Teaches ONE thing | Single framework, fully explained | Covers 5 topics shallowly |
| Each step has an example | "For instance, when I built X..." | Abstract instructions only |
| Case study included | Real numbers, timeline, outcome | "This approach works great" |
| Reader can implement solo | All steps are actionable without outside help | "Contact us for the next step" |
| 3-5 pages | Comprehensive but not padded | 1 page (too thin) or 10 (bloated) |
| Companion post written | LinkedIn post using template | Guide shipped without promotion |
