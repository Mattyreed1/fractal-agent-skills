## Structure

### HOOK (first 200 chars — earns the "see more" click)
This is everything. If this fails, nothing else matters.

Proven patterns from the brand's actual posts:
- **Outcome + timeline**: "It took me 2 years to transition from X to Y. You can do it in 2 days..."
- **Knowledge gap**: "Your best PM's brain is worth more than your project management software. Problem is, most knowledge is stuck in their heads."
- **Bold claim + qualifier**: "{Specific bold statement}. But that's about to change."

Rules:
- Hard line breaks — each line ≤14 words
- Must create a curiosity gap (reader needs to click "see more" to resolve it)
- Specific > vague. Numbers, roles, timelines, outcomes.

### NARRATIVE (body — 800-1,500 chars)
Tell the story or build the argument:
- **Personal experience first**: "I spent a decade in this industry..." / "I'm going thru it in my own business..."
- **Pattern recognition**: Show the old way, then the shift
- **Concrete examples**: Name specific tools, workflows, outcomes
- **Short paragraphs**: 1-3 sentences max, liberal line breaks
- **Arrow notation** (→) for lists of insights or realizations

### TURN (the insight)
The non-obvious lesson or reframe:
- "I keep coming back to one thing..."
- State the principle directly — no "The truth is..." or "Here's why:"
- Make it specific to the audience's situation

### CTA (final lines)
- **Lead magnet**: "Comment '{keyword}' and I'll send you {resource}"
- **Engagement**: "What's your experience with {topic}?"
- **Reframe**: End with the gap/opportunity statement

## Quality Checks

| Check | Pass | Fail |
|-------|------|------|
| Hook earns "see more" | Creates genuine curiosity gap in <200 chars | Vague or fully resolved in hook |
| Story is specific | Names, timelines, tools, outcomes | "I learned a lot from my experience" |
| Paragraphs are short | 1-3 sentences, lots of whitespace | Walls of text |
| Insight is non-obvious | Reader thinks "I hadn't considered that" | Restates common knowledge |
| CTA drives action | Clear next step for the reader | No CTA or generic "thoughts?" |
| Under 2,000 chars | Tight, scannable, fully visible | Truncated or padded |

## Anti-Slop
Per writing skill rules + brand doc:
- No "Excited to share..."
- No "I'm humbled to announce..."
- No "It's not just X, it's Y"
- No "Here's why:" as standalone line
- No "The truth is..."
- No "unlock" / "supercharge" / "game-changer"
- No "in today's fast-paced world"
- Max 1-2 em dashes per post
- No "Let me explain" / "Let's dive in"
